I was born and raised in Romania but my curiousity led me to travel the world. My first adventure started in 2009 when I landed in sunny Cyprus. I spent 4 years working there as a Bartender and enjoyed the sandy beaches and the warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea. My journey continued to the holy land of Guinness and Irish Whiskey where I stayed for 2 years and embraced the Irish culture...and the unpredictable weather... :D. I arrived in London in 2015 and started working as a Cocktail Bartender and, in just over a year I got promoted to Bar Supervisor and Bar Manager. My flat-mate at the time was a Software Developer who ispired me to pursue this path. My first step towards becoming a Web Developer started with FreeCodeCamp, where I had a taste of HTML, CSS and Javascript. I then continued with CodeCademy and took courses on Udemy to further develop my skill set. I have just completed a 12 Week Software Developer Bootcamp organised by _nology and I am excited for the future job opportunities where I can showcase my talent.
